How to play

Every row hides a word, and all the words share a secret connection. The top row hides the word that names that connection.

Drag two-letter fragments from the bank into the empty slots — or tap a fragment, then tap a slot. Tap a placed fragment to send it back.

Nothing is checked until every slot is filled. If the full board isn't right, it shakes — but you won't be told which rows are wrong.

Some fragments are decoys that belong to no word. They'll still be in the bank when you win.

Stuck? The 💡 clue button reveals the whole top word. The 🔎 hint button reveals just one box — tap it, then tap the empty box you want solved.
